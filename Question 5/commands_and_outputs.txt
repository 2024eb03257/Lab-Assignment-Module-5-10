Directory Comparison Script

Setup Commands:
$ mkdir -p dirA dirB
$ echo "Hello World" > dirA/file1.txt
$ echo "Another file in dirA" > dirA/file2.txt
$ echo "This content is the same" > dirA/shared.txt
$ echo "Version A" > dirA/different.txt
$ echo "File only in dirB" > dirB/file3.txt
$ echo "This content is the same" > dirB/shared.txt
$ echo "Version B" > dirB/different.txt

Execution Command:
$ chmod +x sync.sh
$ bash sync.sh dirA dirB

Output:
=== Files only in dirA ===
file1.txt
file2.txt

=== Files only in dirB ===
common2.txt
file3.txt

=== Common files (showing differences) ===
SAME: common.txt
DIFFERENT: same_name.txt
DIFFERENT: subdir/subfile.txt
